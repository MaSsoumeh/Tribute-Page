Professor Maryam Mirzakhani

First woman to win top maths prize

Time line of Maryam Mirazkhani's life:

  • 1977: was born in Tehran, Iran

  • 1994: Mirzakhani became the first Iranian female to win a gold medal at the International Mathematical Olympiad in Hong Kong.

  • 1999: She obtained a Bachelor of Science in mathematics from the Sharif University of Technology

  • 2004: research fellow of the Clay Mathematics Institute and a professor at Princeton University.

  • 2009: She became a professor at Stanford University

  • 2013: Mirzakhani was diagnosed with breast cancer

  • 2014: She was awarded the Fields Medal for "her outstanding contributions to the dynamics and geometry of Riemann surfaces and their moduli spaces"

  • 2017: she died at the age of 40 at Stanford Hospital in Stanford, California
